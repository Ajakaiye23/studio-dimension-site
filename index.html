01 / MISSION — WHO WE ARE

Hand-drawn worlds,
built in new dimensions.

Studio Dimension is an independent animation studio. We believe every frame is a small world — sketched by hand, finished with heart, and engineered with the precision of a machine.

We're a young team of animators, designers and storytellers shaping our debut work. This is where we document the journey — every cut, every artist, every dimension we open.
